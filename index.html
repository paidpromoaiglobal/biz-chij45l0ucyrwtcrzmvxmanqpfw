About Us

Maka Academy, Abhayapuri

Maka Academy is a leading educational institution located in the heart of Abhayapuri, Assam, situated on the 3rd Floor of Auroni Complex on MG Road. We are committed to empowering students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to succeed in their academic and professional journeys. Our dedicated faculty and structured learning environment make us a trusted name in the region.

At Maka Academy, we believe that every student deserves access to quality education. Whether you are preparing for competitive exams, improving your academic performance, or looking to build a strong foundation for your future, our academy offers a supportive and engaging learning experience. With flexible availability throughout the week, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

  • Conveniently located in Abhayapuri Bazar on MG Road, easily accessible to all students
  • Open six days a week with extended hours to accommodate diverse student schedules
  • Experienced and passionate faculty dedicated to student growth and academic excellence
