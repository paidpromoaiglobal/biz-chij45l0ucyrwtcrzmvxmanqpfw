About Us

Maka Academy, Abhayapuri

Maka Academy is a premier educational institution located in Abhayapuri, Assam, situated on the 3rd Floor of the Auroni Complex on MG Road. We are committed to providing accessible, high-quality learning opportunities to students and professionals in the region, offering flexible programs designed to fit every schedule and aspiration.

Nestled in the vibrant Abhayapuri Bazar area, our academy combines experienced faculty with a supportive learning environment to help every student achieve their academic and career goals. Whether you are looking to strengthen your foundation or advance your knowledge, Maka Academy is your trusted partner in education.

  • Conveniently located in Abhayapuri Bazar, easily accessible from across the town
  • Flexible scheduling with extended availability six days a week
  • Dedicated faculty focused on personalized student growth and success
